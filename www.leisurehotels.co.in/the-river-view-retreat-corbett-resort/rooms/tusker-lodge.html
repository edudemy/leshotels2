Tusker Lodge

Are you planning to visit Corbett Park in large groups?  Well, we have an exclusive four-bedroom lodge for you. After a day-long exploration of Corbett Park, make sure you stay in this Lodge for a reposeful night. The sloping tiled roofs, cool stone floors with throw rugs, and block wood furniture create a wild natural ambience for you.

This cottage is recommended for tourists in large groups, but also has an option to be booked by four individuals.
